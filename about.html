Syndicate Security Established

Founded in 2010, Syndicate Security began its journey providing professional on-site protection and manned guarding services across London. With a strong commitment to reliability and client satisfaction, the company quickly earned recognition for its quality service.

Expansion Across London & Watford

By 2017, Syndicate Group had expanded its operations across multiple sites in London and Watford, offering dedicated security personnel and rapid response services. This period marked our transformation into a trusted regional partner for comprehensive security management.

In 2020, we expanded into the field of security training, providing accredited courses to help individuals start or grow their careers in the security industry. This addition allowed Syndicate Group to develop the next generation of professional guards across the UK.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common security service questions.

  • 50+ Licensed Security Officers

1. What security services do you provide?
We offer a complete range of services including manned guarding, mobile patrols, CCTV monitoring, alarm response, and event security — all delivered by licensed and trained professionals.
2. Are your guards SIA licensed and insured?
Yes. Every Syndicate Group officer holds a valid SIA license and full liability insurance. We strictly follow UK security standards and ensure complete compliance for every assignment.
3. Do you offer 24/7 monitoring and emergency support?
Absolutely. Our control room operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing real-time monitoring, rapid response dispatch, and client updates for complete peace of mind.
4. Can you provide security for events or short-term contracts?
Yes. We provide tailored event and temporary security solutions for venues, exhibitions, corporate gatherings, and private functions — with flexible coverage based on your needs.
5. How quickly can your team respond to a call-out?
Our rapid response teams are strategically positioned across the UK to ensure immediate deployment — typically within 15 to 30 minutes depending on location and service type.
