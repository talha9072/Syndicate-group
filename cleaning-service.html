FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about our cleaning services

1. Do you provide after hours or weekend cleaning?
Yes, we offer early morning, late evening and weekend cleaning to ensure your operations run without interruption.
2. Are your cleaners trained and supervised?
All team members receive training in hygiene, safety and cleaning procedures. Supervisors conduct routine inspections to maintain quality.
3. Do you offer deep cleaning and sanitisation?
Yes, we provide deep cleaning, carpet care, upholstery cleaning and periodic full site sanitisation based on your needs.

Yes, we create customised cleaning plans based on your building size, layout and required cleaning frequency.
